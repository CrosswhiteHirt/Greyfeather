Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Overview

Greyfeather is a single-player interactive story game. The current app is designed to run locally with bundled story, image, video, and audio resources.

Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information, create user accounts, ask for names, email addresses, contacts, precise location, photos, or microphone access.

Local Game Data

The game may store progress, branch history, unlocked chapters, endings, and sound settings on your device so that you can continue the story. This data stays on your device and can be removed by deleting the app.

Tracking and Advertising

The current Greyfeather app does not track users across apps or websites and does not use the device advertising identifier for tracking.

Children's Privacy

The game is not intended to collect personal information from children. If you believe personal information has been provided to us, contact us so we can address the issue.

Greyfeather

A Fable of Wings and Silence. A single-player interactive story about a wounded raven, a mountain relay station, and choices that alter trust, truth, pressure, and the ending.

Watch cinematic story scenes, read the clues, and choose who to protect when every message can change the route to the lighthouse.

Key Features

  • Branching interactive story across five acts.
  • Choice-driven state: trust, raven condition, threat pressure, and truth progress.
  • Local cinematic scenes, illustrated chapters, music, and replayable branches.
  • Single-player experience designed for iPhone and iPad.
